# Structural Bridge Note: Activation, Retention, and Reconciliation

**Date:** 2026-03-28

**Purpose:** Public timestamp for the current bridge beyond the constants program. This note is a research-status snapshot, not a full derivation paper.

---

## Claim

The emerging bridge has three distinct layers:

1. **Activation**
   A closed endogenous self-loop can generate nontrivial internal cycling.

2. **Retention**
   A measurement / dephasing channel can preserve pointer sectors while suppressing orthogonal coherences.

3. **Reconciliation**
   A coarse-grained scalar built from activation and retention can define a candidate shared field across many subsystems.

The core point is factorization:

- self-loop alone: activation, no memory
- measurement channel alone: retention, no activation
- composite channel: both

---

## Minimal Structural Picture

### 1. Bare Self-Loop

At the channel level, a bare endogenous loop can carry phase or holonomy without selecting a pointer sector.

Interpretation:
- nontrivial cycling exists
- retained symbol structure does not yet exist

### 2. Bare Measurement Channel

A measurement-like channel can preserve a pointer sector and damp coherence.

Interpretation:
- record stability exists
- internal cycling is absent

### 3. Composite

The composite of self-loop and measurement channel gives:

- protected pointer sector
- decaying coherence sector
- nonzero phase on the decaying sector

This is the first minimal memory-capable structure in the program.

---

## Scalar Bridge

Define:

- **Activation proxy**: phase magnitude on the decaying coherence sector
- **Retention proxy**: contraction strength on that same sector
- **Write-strength**: the product of activation and retention

Write-strength vanishes if either ingredient is missing:

- activation without retention -> zero write-strength
- retention without activation -> zero write-strength
- both together -> nonzero write-strength

This is the candidate local scalar from which a shared reconciliation field may be coarse-grained.

---

## Gravity-Side Interpretation

The current working interpretation is:

- local record-capable systems generate private write events
- a shared field built from many such events is the candidate reconciliation layer
- gravity is the strongest candidate for that shared reconciliation because it is universal

This is not the claim that consciousness causes gravity. The weaker and cleaner claim is:

measurement-like local structure and gravity may instantiate different layers of the same reconciliation architecture.

## What Is Closed vs Open

### Structurally Closed

- activation and retention are not the same thing
- activation alone is insufficient for memory
- retention alone is insufficient for memory
- their composite is the first minimal memory-capable channel
- a write-strength field built from both can be tested for alignment with the shape-space complexity gradient

### Still Open

- derive the activation parameter from puncture-aware holonomy rather than proxy ansatze
- derive the retention parameter from measurement-channel geometry rather than proxy ansatze
- connect the channel-level write-strength to modular first variation without proxies
- prove the coarse-grained field is universal rather than composition-dependent
